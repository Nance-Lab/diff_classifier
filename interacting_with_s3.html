Uploading and download with s3¶

The two basic functions found in the aws package for interacting with s3 are download_s3 and upload_s3. Users must select a bucket by changing the variable bucket_name. Users must also have the correct permissions on their computer to download from or upload to the bucket of interest.

download_s3(remote_fname, local_fname, bucket_name="ccurtis7.pup")
